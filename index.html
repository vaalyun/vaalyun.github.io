Featured project — built solo over 6+ months

Junis — a Thai-speaking AI companion, running locally.

Not a demo — a system that runs every day: she talks in Discord voice calls, remembers people across weeks, plays games with visitors on her website, and streams with her own overlay, donation alerts, and live chat reading.

  • Fine-tuned a 14B-parameter model on a hand-built 822-sample Thai persona dataset — cloud GPU training, then merged and quantized to run on a single consumer graphics card.
  • Long-term memory with measured quality: swapped the embedding model after my eval harness showed Thai retrieval failing 35.6% of the time — now 0.0% on the same benchmark.
  • Real-time voice loop: speech-to-text → reasoning → Thai text-to-speech in Discord calls, with turn-taking and speaker identity.
  • 3D embodiment in Unity — lip-sync, expressions and motion driven by her mind over a local bridge.
  • Streaming stack: OBS overlay suite, Twitch chat ingestion, and a donation pipeline (payment webhook → Cloudflare Worker → on-stream alert + her spoken thank-you).
